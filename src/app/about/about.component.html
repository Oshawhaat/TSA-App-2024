About CyberScouter


This app was made by Alan and Wyatt from FTC team 13737 for TSA Software Development 2024.

It was made to help FTC Robotics teams with scouting other teams at events.

Scouting is a big part of FTC Robotics; knowing which teams pair well with you is crucial for competitive teams.

While this was made for a TSA event, the goal of this app is to help both the Belton robotics teams and any other teams who wish to use our app.
