About CyberScouter


This app was made by Alan and Wyatt for TSA Software Development 2024

It was made for FTC Robotics teams to help with scouting other teams at events

Scouting is a big part of FTC Robotics, knowing which teams pair well with you is crucial for competitive teams

While it was made for a TSA event, Belton's FTC teams will also be using this app to help scout at our robotics events
